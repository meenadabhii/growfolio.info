Common Vehicle Loan Types

Simple Interest Loans

Interest calculated daily on the current principal balance

  • Pay less interest if paid early
  • Most common loan type

Pre-Computed Loans

Interest calculated upfront and added to principal

  • Fixed payment amounts
  • No interest savings for early payoff

Balloon Payment Loans

Smaller monthly payments with large final payment

  • Lower monthly payments
  • Requires planning for final payment
