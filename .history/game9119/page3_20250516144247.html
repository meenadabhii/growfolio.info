Vehicle Loan Features

Everything you need to know about our financing

Comprehensive Loan Types

We offer a complete range of vehicle financing solutions:

  • New Car Loans: Financing up to 100% of MSRP with terms from 24-84 months
  • Used Car Loans: Competitive rates for vehicles up to 7 years old
  • Refinancing: Lower your existing auto loan payments by up to 30%
  • Lease Buyouts: Special programs for converting your lease to ownership
  • Private Party Purchase: Financing for buying from individual sellers

All loans include GAP insurance options and flexible payment date selection.

Interest Rate Factors

Your auto loan rate is determined by several key factors:

  • Credit Score: Rates range from 3.99% for excellent credit (720+) to 14.99% for challenged credit
  • Loan Term: Shorter terms (24-48 months) typically have lower rates than longer terms
  • Vehicle Age: Newer vehicles qualify for better rates than older models
  • Down Payment: Larger down payments (20%+) can secure better interest rates
  • Loan Amount: Loans over $30,000 may qualify for special rate discounts

Our rate match guarantee ensures you get the most competitive financing available.

Documentation Requirements

To process your vehicle loan application, we typically require:

  • Proof of Income: Recent pay stubs (last 30 days) or 2 years of tax returns for self-employed
  • Identification: Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
  • Residence Verification: Utility bill or lease agreement showing current address
  • Insurance Information: Current auto insurance policy or proof of new coverage
  • Vehicle Details: VIN, make, model, year, and mileage for used vehicles

Additional documentation may be required based on your specific financial situation.

Prepayment Benefits & Penalties

Understanding early repayment options:

  • No Prepayment Penalties: Pay off your loan early without any additional fees
  • Interest Savings: Each extra payment reduces total interest paid over the loan term
  • Principal Reduction: Additional payments are applied directly to your principal balance
  • Payment Allocation: Specify whether extra payments should go toward principal or future payments
  • Biweekly Payments: Option to make half-payments every two weeks (26 payments/year)

Early repayment can save hundreds or thousands in interest charges over the loan term.
